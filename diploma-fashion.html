Course Syllabus

Master the art and science of fashion through our 5-module comprehensive curriculum

01

Fashion Illustration

Fundamental drawing, shading, and rendering fashion figures and garments.
  • Basics: Shading, 2D/3D Forms, Value & Pointing
  • Theory: Color Wheel, Tints/Tones, Schemes
  • Principles: Rendering strokes, Motifs, Repeats
  • Croquis: 10-Head Figure, Flesh figure, Poses
  • Categories: Casual, Formal, Ethnic, Beach wear
02

Pattern Making & Construction

Kids Wear
  • Kids Kurthi & Long Frock
  • Lehanga & Jump Suits
  • Basic & Hand Seams
Women's Wear
  • Kurthi & Frocks (Types 1-4)
  • Blouses (7 Styles) & Bottoms
  • Patiala, Palazzo, Straight Pants
03

Embroidery & Styling

  • Basic Stitches: Stem, Chain, Satin, Split
  • Advanced: Zardosi, French Knots, Woven Rose
  • Aari Work: Specialized stitching techniques
  • Hairstyles: French Braid, Buns, Free styles
  • Art: Smocking, Appliqué, Calendar Art
04

Professional Beautician

  • Grooming: High-end Threading, Facial
  • Makeup: Daily & Occasional Make-up Art
  • Saree: Draping (2 styles) & Pre-pleating
  • Henna Art: Professional Mehandi Designs
  • Crafts: Professional Flower making
05

Digital Fashion (AI)

  • Digital: Intro to Adobe Illustrator tools
  • Repeats: Brick, Ogee, Tossed patterns
  • Projects: Flat sketches, Trouser, Blazers
  • Boutique: Logo, Business card, Spec-sheets
  • Effects: Pearl/Sequence Jewelry rendering
